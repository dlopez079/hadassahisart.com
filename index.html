My Message

I am a strong independent woman eager to illustrate a world as I see it through the lens of my eyes. I paint because it allows me to lose myself in pure feeling while my hand waves fluently on paper or on canvas. It is important that the world understands that there is always a way to escape the troubles of reality and bask in the manifestation that all people can create using their mind, body and soul. We call all life free of the chaos of negativity and embrace the positivity that art can bring.

A Little About Me 

Alexandra Jessica Gomez born June 4th 1987 in the city of Windsor Ontario Canada. Much of my childhood leading up to the age of 15 was a blur, so I’ve fast forwarded to years later where I began to write, read, draw, paint and think a lot. In the midst of that I ended up getting married too young to admit I even knew what it was to be a wife. A few months later I added another form of me I didn’t think would happen so fast; I became a mother to a beautiful baby girl.

 It was through those years I battled sickness, battled pain and battled defeat. To be the best version of me. Others would call me Wonder Woman, but I was a mother first, a daughter, a sister, a friend, a god mother and an artist.

Present: Alexandra lives life now thankful for many lessons learned and takes nothing for granted. She works hard as a single mother to make sure the passion she has for life never fades.
